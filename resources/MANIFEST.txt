The icon represents the Copilot How To learning journey with ascending hexagons:

              🔶 MINT
        Advanced Features
        Levels 7-10

          🔶 EMERALD
    Intermediate Skills
    Levels 4-6

      🔶 DARK EMERALD
  Beginner Fundamentals
  Levels 1-3

  • Forest         #047857    Tertiary accent
  • Mint           #6ee7b7    Highlight
